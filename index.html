Why do we have the best investment efficiency?

«Axon» artificial intelligence

TheFund.io is introducing «Axon» artificial intelligence that analyzes key information about projects to invest into:

  • verification of team members;
  • statistic of site visits and user activity;
  • development of our own database;
  • checking for the uniqueness of a White Paper;
  • analysis of competitors;
  • classification of textual data.

Supporting implementation of sponsored projects

TheFund.io provides advisory services and real assistance to the projects to invest into:

  • legal support;
  • economic assistance to the projects;
  • marketing support;
  • establishment of operational teams on monitoring and improving management of project companies;
  • monitoring and support for companies after their entering an exchange.

Multi-signature security system

Multi-signature wallet provides safety for financial assets of the Fund through:

  • reducing the risk of loss of a private key to the wallet;
  • reducing the risk of hacking;
  • setting up a common account;
  • reducing the risk of failure to respect the parties’ interests through joint decision making;
  • monitoring of expenditures collected during the period of ICO;
  • monitoring of a company’s capital flows and capital allocations.

Dividend payments under the smart-contract

Dividend payments under the smart-contract have following advantages:

  • transparency – assets are distributed according to the shares;
  • convenience – there is no bureaucracy, dividend is assessed on the wallets which already contain our tokens;
  • autonomy of decision-making – you manage your assets on your own.
